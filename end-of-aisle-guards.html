End of Aisle Guards

EZ End of Row Guard

Round-end guards with heavy-duty angle for front, side, and rear post protection.

Long Description

Elite Rackz', “Heavy Duty” end of aisle rack protectors, are the ultimate standard for end of a row and tunnel frame guarding. Constructed with 12” tall, ½” thick round ends, to isolate the front frame post from impact damage, as well as 6” x 4” X 3/8″ angle that protects the frame’s side and rear post (highly customizable).

Guards are manufactured to the highest standards with full length inside and outside vertical welds attaching the thick round end to the heavy-duty angle. The angle is punched 12″ on center for 3/4″ X 7″ anchors. Row end guards are available in left side, right side or double side in all lengths over 24″. (Additional punched angle is available to extend to desired overall length.) Notching available on round end and floor angle to accommodate custom applications.

Specifications

  • CATEGORY: Protection
  • SPECIFICATIONS: Standard guard accepts up to a 5 1/2″ wide frame footplate
  • STEEL GAUGE: 10” schedule 60 pipe ½” thick; 6”x 4” ⅜” angle
  • STYLE: Round row end guards are available in left, right or double sided
  • PROTECTOR HEIGHT: 12” tall, ½” thick round ends protect front frame post, 6” x 4” X 3/8″ angle that protects the frame’s side and rear post
  • ROW END GUARD: All lengths over 24” up to 60″ (Additional punched angle available to extend guard length)
  • NUMBER OF ANCHOR HOLES: Varies, spaced less than 12” on center
  • ANCHOR DIMS: ¾” x 7″ included (typically 3)
  • COLOR: Powder coat painted to standard safety yellow
  • PROTECTOR TYPE: Round (standard 42″ and 48″ long guards are available for quick ship)
  • CUSTOMIZABLE: Custom designed for your exact needs, notching and exact lengths to fit your specifications

EZ Pallet Rack Dock Door Guard

Heavy-gauge floor-anchored protection for dock door wall racking.

Long Description

Dock Door Guards protect your valuable all too often abused pallet racking lining the dock door wall. These heavy gauge guards keep forklifts from damaging your racking resulting in expensive emergency repair bills.

The Elite Rackz' dock door guards anchor to the floor for maximum strength and rigidity. The bright yellow safety powder coat paint looks great and is highly visible.

Specifications

  • CATEGORY: Protection
  • SPECIFICATIONS: Standard guard accepts up to a 5 1/2″ wide frame footplate
  • STEEL GAUGE: 1/2” thick steel plate, 6″x4″ 3/8″ angle
